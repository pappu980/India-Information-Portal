Lucknow district is the capital district of Uttar Pradesh and has Lucknow as its headquarters. Lucknow is the administrative headquarters of the eponymous division and district. It is the most populous city in Uttar Pradesh, the 2nd most populous city in northern India and the 11th most populous city in India.

About Lucknow

‡§≤‡§ñ‡§®‡§ä ‡§ï‡•á ‡§¨‡§æ‡§∞‡•á ‡§Æ‡•á‡§Ç

Lucknow, the capital of Uttar Pradesh, stands at an elevation of 123 meters above sea level and is located in the heart of the state. The city has always been a multicultural city that flourished as a North Indian cultural and artistic hub, and the seat of power of Nawabs in the 18th and 19th centuries.
